M&M Ice Company

A family run and operated business since 1999,
delivering quality ice in the greater Wisconsin Dells area.

Who We Are

M&M Ice has been a family business since 1999 with happy and satisfied customers since our first ice bag. Being one of the largest family own ice businesses in our area is a great honor.

Where We Are

Located in the heart of Wisconsin, we take pride in our roots. Being able to give our clients their ice in a timely manner is what leads our company forward in the competitive state of Wisconsin.

What We Can Give

Having the advantage of being in the heart of Wisconsin, our timely delivery system has had the ability to excel. If you are within 50 miles of Wisconsin Dells, we can provide for you.

What we do

Being in business for 22 years, we had the ability to broaden our original starting product from just the familiar 20 Pounder, to now over 3 different types of products for all your needs.

7lb Ice Bag

Our most beloved personal serving of ice for any small get together you may have.

20lb Ice Bag

Our most frequently purchased 20lb bag is great for filling most coolers up to the perfect level to allow for drinks to also be added in.

Block Ice

A 12x8in solid block of ice for any other kind of cooling you need!
